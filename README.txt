Integrate your Grünbeck water softener from the softliQ-SD series in Homey.
Monitor the remaining capacity and the salt supply. Let Homey notify you of any changes.

First steps:
* Enter your Grünbeck login data in the app settings
* Set the polling interval to the desired period
* Activate the periodic request
* Add your softliQ-SD water softener as a new device in Homey
* After filling up with salt, reset the salt fill level in device settings/maintenance or per flow to the desired value
